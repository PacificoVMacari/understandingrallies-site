UR Logger — now available on the Microsoft Store.

UR Logger is the first app in the URSquash suite by Understanding Rallies. Log matches live or from video footage and generate instant shot and rally analysis — all stored locally on your device.

Current release

  • Desktop app for squash rally logging and analysis.
  • Log matches live courtside or from video footage.
  • Automatic shot profile and winning rally breakdown generated after every session.
  • All data stored locally — no account required.
  • Available now on the Microsoft Store for Windows.

Privacy policy.

Understanding Rallies is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all apps published by Understanding Rallies, including UR Logger.

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

UR Logger does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All match data recorded by the app is saved locally on your device in your Documents folder and never leaves your device.

Data collected

UR Logger does not collect any personal data. The app creates and stores the following files locally on your device only:

  • Match event logs (JSON and CSV files) saved to your Documents folder
  • Analysis output images generated from your session data
  • A keymap preferences file storing your custom keyboard shortcuts
  • A first-launch flag file used to show the tutorial on first use

None of these files are uploaded to Understanding Rallies or any third party.

Video files

Any video files you choose to analyse remain entirely on your device. UR Logger processes video locally using FFmpeg and OpenCV. No video footage is transmitted or stored by Understanding Rallies.

Analytics and tracking

UR Logger does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Third-party software

UR Logger bundles the following open source libraries. These operate entirely locally and do not transmit data:

  • FFmpeg — video processing (LGPL 2.1)
  • Qt / PySide6 — user interface (LGPL 3.0)
  • OpenCV — video analysis (Apache 2.0)
  • NumPy and pandas — data processing (BSD)

Full license notices are included within the installed application.

Future versions

Future versions of UR Logger and other Understanding Rallies apps may introduce optional account features for subscription management. If personal data is collected at that point, this policy will be updated before release and users will be informed clearly.
