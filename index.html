UR Coaching Planner — plan, review, and grow your drill library.

UR Coaching Planner is built for squash coaches who want one place to organise session plans, store drill notes, review sessions, manage squads, and keep their coaching philosophy visible throughout the season.

Designed around the coaching workflow

Build a session from warm-up to match play, attach drills to each block, review what happened afterwards, and keep player or squad notes connected to your planning process.

  • Plan: create structured sessions using a drill library and session builder.
  • Calendar: view planned sessions by date and reopen them when needed.
  • Review: write post-session reflections, player notes, and improvement points.
  • Squads: organise groups, profiles, levels, and session history.
  • Philosophy: store your coaching values, approach, and player-development goals.

Privacy policy.

Understanding Rallies is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all apps published by Understanding Rallies, including UR Logger and UR Coaching Planner.

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Understanding Rallies apps do not collect, transmit, or store personal information. Match data, coaching sessions, reviews, squad notes, and drill information are saved locally on your device and never leave your device.

Data collected

Understanding Rallies apps do not collect any personal data. The apps create and store the following files locally on your device only:

  • Match event logs (JSON and CSV files) saved to your Documents folder
  • Analysis output images generated from your session data
  • A keymap preferences file storing your custom keyboard shortcuts
  • A first-launch flag file used to show the tutorial on first use
  • Coaching Planner session plans, reviews, squad profiles, coaching philosophy notes, goals, and custom drill entries

None of these files are uploaded to Understanding Rallies or any third party.

Video files

Any video files you choose to analyse remain entirely on your device. UR Logger processes video locally using FFmpeg and OpenCV. No video footage is transmitted or stored by Understanding Rallies.

Analytics and tracking

UR Logger does not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Third-party software

UR Logger bundles the following open source libraries. These operate entirely locally and do not transmit data:

  • FFmpeg — video processing (LGPL 2.1)
  • Qt / PySide6 — user interface (LGPL 3.0)
  • OpenCV — video analysis (Apache 2.0)
  • NumPy and pandas — data processing (BSD)

Full license notices are included within the installed application.

Future versions

Future versions of UR Logger, UR Coaching Planner, and other Understanding Rallies apps may introduce optional account features for subscription management. If personal data is collected at that point, this policy will be updated before release and users will be informed clearly.
